About

With only 104 luxurious Apartments, Atharva Elanza is the essence of community living, here, exclusivity is found in dramatically limited units and genuine friendship is struck in an enclave of like-minded neighbors. Luxurious Apartments exude a modern resplendence and a statement which says 'You have arrived!'.

Be one with nature at Atharva Elanza in koba Gandhinagar. Every facet of the bunglow is a sheer symphony with nature's bounty, be it the never-ending Nature facing views or the amazing landscape of greenery and vast open spaces, Enjoy the breathing in rejuvenating pure fresh air with Nature as you next-door-neighbor.

Basking in stark affluence, embracing many a world, inspiring many people! Welcome to a world perfect in virtually every respect. 3 BHK Luxurious Apartments with spacious and abundant open landscape spaces and a delightful clubhouse that features a host of premium leisure amenities in Gandhinagar.

Never before has, and perhaps never again in our time, will so many exceptional elements be put together to create such a remarkable residential landmark... and more importantly, to create the holistic experience that the limited edition residential units offer-for fewer privileged ones!
